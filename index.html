About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher working on machine learning for medical imaging at Martinos Center, under the supervision of Matt Rosen and Juan Eugenio Iglesias.

Before joining Martinos Center, I worked on deep learning for medical image analysis and got my Ph.D. degree (Summa Cum Laude) from School of Computer Science at Technical University of Munich, supervised by Bjoern Menze. During my Ph.D., I visited Daniel Rueckert's lab and Department of Quantitative Biomedicine at UZH for two years.
