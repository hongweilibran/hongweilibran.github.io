I currently work on medical image analysis and machine learning with Matt Rosen and Juan Eugenio Iglesias at Martinos center, with a focus on low-field MRI.

During my PhD program, I was supervised by Bjoern Menze, working closedly with Benedikt Wiestler at TUM. I obtained my M.Sc. degree in Informatics at Sun Yat-sen University incl. an eight-month exchange at CVIP group at the University of Dundee, supervised by Wei-Shi Zheng and Jianguo Zhang.

From time to time, I look for self-motivated students to explore research ideas and document the outcome. If you are interested in working with me, please send me an email about your background and research statements.
