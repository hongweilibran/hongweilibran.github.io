I currently lead a health informatics computing team at School of Biomedical Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU). Before joining SYSU, I worked at Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology(CAS) as an associate professor from 2010 to 2018. I also worked in Prof Peter Hunter's team in University of Auckland New Zealand as a postdoc from 2007 to 2010. I obtained the Ph.D. in medical image comuting from Hong Kong Univeristy of Science and Technology at 2007, and M.Eng. and B.Sc. in Engineering Physics from Tsinghua University China.

I always look for highly-motivated students with strong mathematical background or excellent coding skills. If you are interested in working with me, please send me an email about your interests and background (attaching your CV, transcripts, and any previous research papers).
